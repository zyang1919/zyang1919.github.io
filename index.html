About

I am an Assistant Teaching Professor in the Department of Statistics at Penn State. My interests include Bayesian Functional Data Analysis (BFDA), survival modeling, outcome-dependent follow-up, and statistical computing in R. I develop research software and create course materials for probability and stochastic modeling.
