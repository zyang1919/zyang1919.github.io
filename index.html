About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematical Sciences (concentration in Statistics) from Northern Illinois University in 2025. My research interests include Bayesian analysis, survival analysis, longitudinal analysis, and statistical computing in R. I am also interested in modeling the electronic health record (EHR) and DNA methylation data. I am currently an Assistant Teaching Professor in the Department of Statistics at The Pennsylvania State University.
