About Me

I am an Assistant Teaching Professor in the Department of Statistics at Penn State. My research interests include Bayesian Data Analysis, survival anaylsis, bioinformatics, and statistical computing in R. I am also interested in modeling electronic health record (EHR) and DNA methylation data.
