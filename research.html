Research

My work focuses on Bayesian modeling for functional and longitudinal data, with applications to survival analysis, outcome-dependent follow-up, and high-dimensional biomedical settings.

Topics

Bayesian Functional Data Analysis (BFDA)

Multivariate smoothing splines, MMCMC, and inference for functional outcomes.

Mixture Models in Survival Analysis

α-mixture survival models and related Bayesian hierarchies.

Outcome-Dependent Follow-Up

Discrete-time follow-up processes and bias-aware inference.

Statistical Computing

Reproducible workflows and research software in R.
